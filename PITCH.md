# TransitFlow
### Run your commuter transport business from one app.
**Live: https://transitflow-ten.vercel.app**

Today you manage transport with WhatsApp, cash, and spreadsheets - chasing
payments, answering "where is the bus?" calls, guessing which routes make money.
TransitFlow replaces that with one platform.

## What each side gets

**Your riders (free app)**
- Pay their monthly fare via your Stripe/Ziina link - money goes straight to you
- Cash riders tap "I have paid"; you confirm with one click
- Watch their bus live on a map; get departed/arrived alerts
- Automatic payment reminders before, on, and after the due date

**Your drivers (one-tap console)**
- Open app, tap Start trip - the phone becomes the GPS tracker. No hardware.
- One tap per stop notifies every rider on the route

**You (dashboard)**
- Live map of every bus on the road
- Who paid, who's overdue - full roster with one-click confirmation
- Fuel, maintenance & cost tracking per bus; revenue minus costs = real profit
- Your bus names, your routes, your fares; announcements reach all riders instantly

## Setup: one afternoon
Create company > add buses & routes > share one invite code (riders sign
themselves up) > drivers start their first trip. No hardware, no training -
works on any phone.
