# TransitFlow
### Run your commuter transport business from one app.

**Live at: https://transitflow-ten.vercel.app**

---

## The problem

If you run staff or commuter transport today, you probably manage it with
WhatsApp messages, cash, and spreadsheets:

- Chasing riders every month to find out who has paid and who hasn't
- Answering "where is the bus?" calls every single morning
- No clean record of what each bus costs you in fuel and maintenance
- No way to know which routes actually make you money

TransitFlow replaces all of that with one platform: an app for your riders,
a console for your drivers, and a dashboard for you.

---

## How it works - three sides, one system

### 1. Your riders get an app
Riders join with a single invite code you share. From their phone they can:

- **Pay their monthly fare** - through your own Stripe or Ziina payment link,
  so the money goes directly to your account, never through us
- **Report offline payments** - if they pay cash or bank transfer, they tap
  "I have paid" and you confirm it with one click
- **Watch the bus live on a map** - real-time GPS position of their bus,
  so they stop calling to ask where it is
- **Get automatic alerts** - bus departed, arrived at their stop, picked up,
  dropped off - plus payment reminders before, on, and after the due date
- **See their route details** - departure time, their pickup stop, running days

### 2. Your drivers get a one-tap console
No training needed. The driver opens the app, picks the route, taps
**Start trip**, and their phone begins broadcasting the bus location.
One tap for "arrived at stop" or "passengers picked up" notifies every
rider on that route instantly. No GPS hardware to buy or install -
the driver's phone is the tracker.

### 3. You get the business dashboard
- **Live fleet map** - every bus currently on the road, in real time
- **Who paid, who didn't** - full rider roster with payment status,
  next due date, pickup stop, and home area of every rider
- **One-click payment confirmation** - rider-reported payments queue up
  for you to approve; confirming automatically extends their subscription
  and notifies them
- **Overdue chase list** - riders past their due date, front and centre,
  with a call button
- **Cost tracking & accounting** - log fuel, maintenance, salaries,
  insurance per bus; see revenue minus costs = real profit per month
- **Routes & buses your way** - name buses how you already do
  ("Bus 28", "VIP Coach"), build routes with ordered stops, set the
  monthly fare per route
- **Announcements** - post a route change or holiday notice once;
  every rider is notified instantly

---

## Money flows directly to you

This matters: TransitFlow never holds your money. You paste your own
Stripe or Ziina payment link into your settings, riders pay through it,
and the funds land in your account the same way they do today. Cash and
bank transfer riders are tracked in the same dashboard, so you have one
complete picture regardless of how each rider pays.

---

## Getting started takes one afternoon

1. **Create your company** - 2 minutes, name + city + currency
2. **Add your buses and routes** - labels, stops, departure times, monthly fare
3. **Share your invite code** - one code, by WhatsApp or printed on the bus;
   riders sign up themselves (email or Google) and pick their route and stop
4. **Drivers start their first trip** - tracking and alerts work immediately

No hardware. No installation. No training sessions. Works on any phone
through the browser and installs to the home screen like a normal app.
